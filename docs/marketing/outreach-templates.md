# Outreach Templates

These templates are for light, non-spammy outreach to small DeFi/token teams. The goal is not to sell an audit. The goal is to offer a practical launch-readiness pass and collect real feedback.

---

## Discord / Telegram short DM

Hi — I’m building a lightweight launch-readiness toolkit for DeFi/token projects.

It checks common pre-audit issues like owner/admin privileges, mint/pause/blacklist/tax controls, public state-changing functions, deployment readiness and CI readiness.

It is not a formal audit, but it can help teams prepare better before audit or mainnet launch.

If useful, I can run a quick readiness pass on your public repo and send a short report.

GitHub:
https://github.com/moseszhu999/defi-launch-safety-kit

---

---

## Rules

Do not spam.

Do not claim to provide a formal audit unless a proper audit scope and process exists.

Do not publicly accuse a live project of having vulnerabilities.

Start with public repos, demo projects, hackathon projects and teams explicitly asking for feedback.
