## Decision logic

### Strong signal

Continue current direction if any of these happened:

- 3+ meaningful replies
- 1 team asks for a readiness note
- 1 founder asks for a call
- 1 public issue/discussion conversation opens
- 1 paid readiness review becomes plausible
- 1 reusable case study can be written

### Weak but useful signal

Adjust messaging if:

- likes/profile views happened, but no replies
- people understand the post but do not ask for a report
- target teams are mostly learning repos or inactive projects

### No signal

Reposition or change target segment if:

- no replies
- no profile views
- no GitHub movement
- no target team looks credible
