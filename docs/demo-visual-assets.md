## Suggested caption

A lightweight launch-readiness report should not replace a formal audit.

But before spending audit budget, small DeFi/token teams should already know:
- who can mint
- who can pause
- who can blacklist
- who can change tax/fees
- who can rescue or withdraw funds
- whether tests, deployment scripts, and CI gates exist

That is the gap DeFi Launch Safety Kit tries to cover.
